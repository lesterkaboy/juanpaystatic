Our Company

"The ACM Group of Companies is a dynamic and diversified conglomerate established in 2022, built on a strong foundation of excellence, integrity, and innovation. With a clear vision to create sustainable business solutions and contribute to national development, ACM has rapidly expanded its presence across various sectors including logistics, financial services, franchising, real estate, construction, and business process outsourcing. At its core, ACM Group is committed to empowering Filipino entrepreneurs and communities by providing high-quality services, innovative platforms, and inclusive business opportunities. Through strategic partnerships and a people-centered approach, ACM fosters long-term value not only for its clients and stakeholders but also for the broader public. Driven by a leadership team with a forward-thinking mindset and a passion for nation-building, the ACM Group continues to grow with a mission to uplift lives, modernize traditional industries, and bridge gaps in service delivery—especially for the underserved and unserved segments of the population."
